Suzanne Aubert

Who Is She?

Suzanne Aubert was a religious sister who was also known as Mother Aubert who started a home for orphans and the under-privileged in Jerusalem, New Zealand on the Whanganui River in 1885.
Suzanne Aubert was born on June 19 1835 in Saint-Symphorien-de-lay, France, She was a daughter in a family of 6, having 3 brothers named Alphonse, Louis and Camille.
Suzanne Aubert carried out her mission in New Zealand, before she got to New Zealand by sea tho, she learnt Te Reo on the ship.
